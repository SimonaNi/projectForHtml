About



Mythological Cowboys is an RPG for PC, Mac, Linux, Nintendo Switch and Xbox One.

In this game, you can switch between two characters, each presenting different fighting options and ailities.

The story revolves around two drifters, who eventiually become bounty hunters for finantial reasons. The public knows them as "Jack J Millson" and "ap Saul". They travel and cross paths with mythological creatures and odd occurances from all over the land.



Characters:

Jack J Millson is the main character of the story. He is a bounty hunter who meets his companion on a trip to hourseback mountain and "team up" out of convinience.
His weapons are firearms, lasos and explosives. He is a longrange and shortrange fighter (if needed).




Ap. Saul aka the vampire hunter of Springston, is the travaling companion of Jack JM. He used to travel with a group of mercinaries that called themselves "The Appostles" or "God's Judgment" before they all died - leeaving him the soul surviver.
He specialises in non-human "handling". His first solo job was a vampire infestation in Springston, which earned him his nickname.
